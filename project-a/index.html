Digital Jellyfish

Scientific Name:

Aurelia aurita

Discovered by / at:

Stephen Gary Wozniak, Release of the first personal computer of Apple,1976

Family:

A small branch called Medusozoa of the tree of life in digital monitors, where these species eat trash folders and small viruses.

Habitat:

Digital spaces without viruses and usually no one visits. Prefer to live in thosespaces with electronic corals.

Appearance:

Due to the transparency, we can only see the colorful skeletons, where circles nested in each other. Several wings and legs to help it move around in the water.

Key Characteristics:

  • Float away when someone is near
  • Rest in corals
  • Constantly changing colors
  • Shrink when attacked
